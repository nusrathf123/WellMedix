Recommended Games to De-Stress

Boost your mood and refresh your mind with these calming and fun games.

1. Bubble Wrap Popper

  • A simple clickable pop game.
  • Designed to calm your mind with satisfying pops.

2. Memory Card Game

  • Activates the brain in a low-pressure way, improving concentration.
  • Boosts mood by completing small tasks successfully.

3. Fun Board Games

  • Like Snakes and Ladders, Ludo, etc.
  • Turns stress relief into a fun daily habit, helpful for medical professionals.

4. Puzzles

  • Improves focus, patience, and visual-spatial skills.
  • Great for solo or group play with family/friends.

5. Candy Crush

  • Popular match-three puzzle game.
  • Stress-relieving and enjoyable experience.

6. Calm Jenga

  • Physical skill game requiring concentration and steady hands.
  • Promotes mindfulness and diverts attention from stressors.
