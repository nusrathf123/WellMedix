About Us

In today’s fast-paced world, especially for medical burnout professionals and individuals in high-stress environments, burnout has become increasingly common. We understand that everyone experiences stress differently and that’s why we created this platform.

We are a team of passionate students committed to creating a meaningful impact through technology. This project was built as part of InnovateX 3.0, blending health tech with AI — innovative to address real-world problems.
