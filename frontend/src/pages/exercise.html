EXERCISES

Breathing & Relaxation

  • Box Breathing: Inhale for 4 seconds.
  • Exhale for 4 seconds.
  • Hold for 4 seconds.
  • Repeat for 1–4 minutes.

Stretching & Mobility

  • Neck rolls: slowly roll your neck clockwise and counterclockwise.
  • Shoulder shrugs: lift shoulders to ears, hold for 3 seconds, release.

Mindfulness & Meditation

  • 5-Minute Meditation: close your eyes, focus on your breath, let thoughts pass.
  • Body Scan Relaxation: lie down, mentally scan head to toe, relaxing each muscle.

Yoga

  • Child’s pose:
  • Sit on your heels, knees apart.
  • Extend arms forward, rest forehead on the floor.
  • Breathe deeply and hold for 30 seconds to 1 minute.

Visualization

  • Close your eyes and imagine a peaceful scene (e.g., forest, mountain).
  • Engage senses—sounds, smells, sensations.
  • Breathe deeply and immerse for 5–10 minutes.

Laughter Therapy

  • Start with a forced smile and fake laughter.
  • Watch a funny video or recall a happy memory.
  • Engage in social moments that spark joy and laughter.

Relaxation Exercise

  • Tense muscles for 5 seconds, then release.
  • Move upwards, focusing on each release.

Quick Physical Activity

  • 5-Minute Cardio: jumping jacks, high knees, or a quick jog.
  • Chair Squats: stand and sit repeatedly for 1 minute.
